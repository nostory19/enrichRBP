Welcome to enrichRBP

Welcome to our web server for circRNA-RBP and LinearRNA-RBP binding site recognition. This server provides a convenient and user-friendly platform for performing state-of-the-art identification methods for these binding sites. With our online prediction module, you can apply up to 10 different methods to your data and analyze the results in detail using our result analysis module. Our server also allows you to evaluate and compare the performance of the different methods in terms of prediction accuracy, Motif analysis, interpretable analysis, and more. Whether you are a researcher, a student, or just curious about these binding sites, our web server offers a comprehensive and versatile tool for your binding site recognition needs.

Imputation

Our online prediction module of circRNA-RBP and linearRNA-RBP allow users to apply up to 10 different state-of-the-art methods for the identification of these binding sites, providing a convenient and user-friendly platform for researchers and students alike. The module also offers three key functions: probabilistic prediction, Motif analysis, and interpretability analysis.

Downstream Analysis

Downstream Analysis includes probability assessments of RBP binding sites in the sequences, as well as graphical and textual presentations of motif analysis and interpretable analysis. Probabilistic prediction allows users to evaluate the performance of different methods in terms of prediction accuracy, and motif analysis allows users to identify the underlying patterns in binding sites, providing valuable insights into their characteristics and functions. Finally, interpretability analysis allows users to gain a further understanding of the mechanisms behind binding site recognition, allowing users better understand the underlying biological processes.
